include machines memory image, in two parts:
    - instructions image
    - data image 

----------------------text lines----------------------
first line -> 
total length of instructions (by encoded words count)
total length of data (by encoded words count)

every line: 
word address    word content

all numbers will be in base4 (a,b,c,d)

the content of the word will be change according to the AER status
A will be set regular
R -> the content is depended on the label's address
E will be handled by the linker
